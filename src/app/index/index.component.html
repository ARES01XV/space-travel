The Medusa
Nebula

This is a planetary nebula in the constellation of Gemini. It is also known as Abell 21 and Sharpless 2-274.

The Bubble
Nebula

This is an H II region emission nebula in the constellation Cassiopeia. It lies close to the direction of the open cluster Messier 52.

Star Formation in
the Eagle Nebula

Gravity pulls clouds of gas together to collapse inward. With enough gas, nuclear fusion ignites in the center, creating a shining star.
